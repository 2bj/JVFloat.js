Jvfloat.js

jQuery / Zepto plugin to emulate Matt D. Smith's floating placeholder text

Demo

jQuery and Zepto plugin to emulate the behavior of JVFloatLabeledTextField which based on the concept from Matt D. Smith.

Howto

  • place jQuery.js or zepto.js, jvfloat.js, and jvfloat.css on your <HEAD>.
  • use jvFloat() functions to enable JVFloat.js functionality on your input element.
